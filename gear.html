My Loadout

My Computer

So I use a lot of different things everyday, so here is my loadout, My computer is a 2012 MacBook Pro, with 240gb of storage, and 8gb of ram. Running macOS Monterey through a patcher, it's kinda slow but it runs. I am considering upgrading to one of the new 14" M1 Pro Macbooks. Though a 13" M1 would be plenty for my workload.

My Phone

My phone is the 128gb model of the the iPhone Xr. I love this thing, it's got great battery life letting me use it all day without charging it as long as I plug it in before I go to bed. Plus it's strong enough so that I dont have to use a case with out it breaking.

My Headphones

My daily driver headphones are the Airpods Pro. Love these things! The noise cancellation is great and the sound quality is amazing.

My Watch

As I use both Airpods, an iPhone, and a Macbook Pro, what watch do you think I use? That's right I use the Apple Watch Series 3. I love being able to track my movement, answer calls and texts, and so much more from the ease of my watch.
