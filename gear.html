My Loadout

My Computer

So I use a lot of different things everyday, so here is my loadout, My computer is a Lenovo Thinkpad X1 Carbon G2. With 240gbs of storage and 8gbs of ram this thing does the trick for now. No need to upgrade anytime soon.

My Phone

My phone is the 128gb model of the the iPhone 13 Pro. I love this thing, it's got great battery life letting me use it all day without charging it as long as I plug it in before I go to bed.

My Headphones

My daily driver headphones are the Airpods Pro. Love these things! The noise cancellation is great and the sound quality is amazing.

My Watch

As I use both Airpods and an iPhone so what watch do you think I use? That's right I use the Apple Watch Series 3. I love being able to track my movement, answer calls and texts, and so much more from the ease of my watch.
